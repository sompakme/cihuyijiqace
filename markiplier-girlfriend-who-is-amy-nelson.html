Markiplier, whose real name is Mark Edward Fischbach, is an American YouTuber, gamer, actor, and philanthropist.

Markiplier is best known for his YouTube channel, where he has over 30 million subscribers as of 2021.

He initially started his channel in 2012, posting videos of himself playing video games, and has since expanded to vlogging, sketch comedy, and other types of content.

Aside from his YouTube channel, Markiplier has also worked on several acting projects. He has appeared in TV shows such as “The Game Theorists” and “Five Nights at Freddy’s: The Musical,” as well as films like “Smash!” and “The Big Break.”

Markiplier is also known for his philanthropic work. He has raised millions of dollars for various charities through his live streams and other fundraising efforts.

In 2018, he launched his own charity, the Markiplier Foundation, which supports organizations that focus on mental health and education.

Markiplier has won numerous awards for his work, including a Shorty Award for Best Gaming Channel in 2016 and a Streamy Award for Best Gaming Channel in 2019.
