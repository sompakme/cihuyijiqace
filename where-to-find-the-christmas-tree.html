Where to find the Christmas tree?

August 2024 · 5 minute read

In NBA 2K23's MyCareer mode, the holiday season is in full swing, and the popular Christmas Tree feature has returned.

In the past, the Christmas Tree has been a source of generous rewards for players, often giving out hundreds of thousands of VC (virtual currency). However, this year's holiday festivities in the game have been different for players on different platforms. Still, the tree gives gamers access to multiple free rewards that can help them gain an advantage in different game modes.

NBA 2K23 Gamers who are not on next-gen consoles might feel hard done by because the Christmas tree rewards have not been made available to them.

NBA 2K23: Where to find the Christmas tree for extra rewards?

As mentioned, current-gen and next-gen gamers have a few differences when it comes to the Christmas tree rewards this year.

In the current-gen mode of NBA 2K23, gamers are able to gain the rewards by visiting the Christmas tree present by the Deuces Courts on the Platinum Deck. Gamers who have already accessed these rewards know that it includes a Snowman T-shirt, as well as 10 rebounding boosts that can be used in different game modes.

On the other hand, next-gen players were given a 12 Days of Giving Locker Codes promotion instead of the traditional Christmas Tree. The 12 days of locker codes have included some top-notch rewards, including various Christmas-themed accessories and other holiday gear.

Various gamers and Christmas challenges were also present in the City for next-gen gamers. This means that NBA 2K23 gamers who are on a next-gen console have a range of extra rewards that they can gain.

Current-gen players still have access to the Festive Balling event, which has a different in-game event planned every day until New Year's Day. These events, as well as the free locker code, can also be used by current-gen players in order to get ahead.

Still, NBA 2K23 is firmly tilted towards next-gen users and has given them access to various extra rewards. Gamers who frequent the MyNBA mode may as well consider upgrading to next-gen to access these events and promotions.

Regardless, the free locker code is available to all gamers and guarantees a pink diamond. The players available are Klay Thompson, Lonzo Ball, Draymond Green, Dwight Howard and John Wall.
