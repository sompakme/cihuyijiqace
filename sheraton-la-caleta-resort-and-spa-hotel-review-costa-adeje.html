Sheraton La Caleta Resort and Spa Hotel Review, Costa Adeje

July 2024 · 2 minute read

Enviably located in Costa Adeje in southern Tenerife, this five star hotel and spa is the perfect place to relax and recharge. Within walking distance from Golf Costa Adeje, the natural beauty that surrounds the hotel is undeniable.

The luxurious rooms and suites, in and outdoor resort amenities and selection of restaurants all contribute to the indulgent experience.

Andrea Montgomery, Telegraph’s Destination Expert says:“ Hushed relaxation and an air of exclusivity amongst the palm trees are the hallmarks of this stylish Sheraton. Old-school professional service, understated opulence and luxuriant surroundings provide the perfect de-stress getaway." 

What’s included:        

  • Return flight from selected UK airports (regional airports on request)
  • Half board
  • Return airport transfers
  • Hold luggage can be added from £45 per person per bag

2019 prices:

Five nights – May £899pp, June £959pp, July £1129pp, August £1079pp, September £999pp, October £1039pp.

We also love....

The style

Sandstone turrets, sweeping staircases and softly rounded columns pay homage to Tenerife's proximity to North Africa. 

The food

Breakfast by the pool is a delicious way to start the day. An impressive range and quality of choice is provided in the dinner buffet which also features theme nights. 

The service

Professional, attentive staff at all levels. Reception can incur a wait at busy times.
