Belgium vs San Marino ended 9-0 in favour of the Red Devils as they became the first country to qualify for Euro 2020.

Romelu Lukaku scored a brace to reach his landmark of 50 international goals for his country as he opened the scoring in the 28th minute.

And the Inter Milan striker completed his brace four minutes before half time after Nacer Chadli and an own goal from Cristian Brolli increased the goal margin.
