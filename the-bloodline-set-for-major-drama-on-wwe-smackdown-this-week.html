The Bloodline Set For Major Drama On WWE SmackDown This Week

August 2024 · 2 minute read

Roman Reigns is still leading the Bloodline, even though he is reportedly taking long hiatus soon as champion. Jimmy and Jey Uso have disappointed the Tribal Chief time and time again, but it seems there is no end to that.

We previously reported a few spoilers for SmackDown this week. You can click here to find out WWE’s plan for the US Title. This goes without saying, but this is also your official spoiler warning for the rest of the article.

BWE, who has a proven track record of getting insider WWE reports correct behind his private Twitter account, tweeted out a few spoilers for SmackDown. In those spoilers, he included a very interesting note about WWE’s plan for the Bloodline this week.

Usos will F up and Roman wont be happy

It was not notes how the Usos will “F up,” but Roman Reigns doesn’t take kindly to failure. Only time will tell how much longer things have before everything comes to a hear.

Roman Reigns and Solo Sikoa are also set to take on Kevin Owens and Sami Zayn in an Undisputed Tag Team Title match at Night of Champions on May 27th. That match could add even more drama to the Bloodline’s ongoing saga.
