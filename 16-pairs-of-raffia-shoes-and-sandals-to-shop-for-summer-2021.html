16 Pairs of Raffia Shoes and Sandals to Shop For Summer 2021

July 2024 · 4 minute read

Out of all the seasons, summer in particular allows for much more variety when it comes to getting dressed. Without being so hung up on practicality and warmth, we're finally free to fully enjoy some of the year's most fun and exciting trends — especially if we're talking about footwear. For 2021, raffia shoes, with their summery nature that feels reminiscent of resort wear, are an instant way to take your feet on a tropical vacation, even if the farthest you actually get is a long scroll through travel TikTok.

Where there's a reigning fashion trend, there's often foreshadowing on the runways, and this classic shoe was spotted all over at Spring/Summer 2021 collections. There were chunky raffia flatforms at Altuzarra, which were paired with everything from trousers to airy sundresses, while a flat, strappy raffia sandal debuted at Christian Dior in both black and natural shades.

As we head into the heat, this palm tree-derived material is popping up at some of our favorite stores, and has even been married with other shoe trends of the moment: think heeled thong sandals with raffia soles, as well as options complete with floral accents, playing up the whimsical garden theme that's also pretty popular. These small updates make it feel as if we're being introduced to timeless trend all over again.

Ahead, we've rounded up 16 of the best raffia shoes we could find — flats, heels and all.
