Warframe Whispers in the Walls: Release Date, Story, Weapons, Factions, More.

Here's everything you need to know about Warframe: Whispers in the Walls, including its release date, story, weapons, factions, and more.

First Published December 4, 2023, 10:15

Warframe: Whispers in the Walls is the next story expansion for Warframe. It adds a new hub area, story missions, secondary weapon type, factions, and more to the game. 

At TennoCon 2023, Digital Extremes officially unveiled Whispers in the Walls with an extensive 30-minute demo. Unlike this year's Duviri Paradox, this upcoming expansion has a more retro vibe, with cell phones, CRT monitors, and strange characters setting the stage for what could be a noir mystery. 

Like previous Warframe expansions, Whispers in the Walls has plenty to offer. This guide covers all the latest news on Warframe: Whispers in the Walls, including key details like its release date, story, weapons, and more.

4 December 2023: Updated with all the latest information on Warframe's upcoming expansion Whispers in the Walls.

Table of Contents

Warframe: Whispers In The Walls Latest News

24 November 2023: Whispers In The Walls Adds Qorvex Warframe

In addition to new story missions, secondary weapon type, and plenty of rewards, Whispers in the Walls introduces the game's 55th Warframe, Qorvex. 

This brute frame was designed by Albercht Entrati himself to protect the Entrati lab from all kinds of sinister forces. Qorvex specializes in dealing AOE Radioactive damage. You can get his blueprints from the new Cavia Syndicate coming with Whispers in the Walls. 

24 November 2023: Whispers In The Walls Teaser Trailer Revealed

Digital Extremes premiered a short teaser trailer for Whispers in the Walls during its latest devstream 175. The full trailer will be released at The Game Awards 2023 alongside the expansion's release date. 

Warframe: Whispers in the Walls Release Date

Warframe Whispers in the Walls will be released in the winter of 2023. A concrete release date will be announced at The Game Awards on December 7. 

Warframe: Whispers In The Walls Story

Whispers in the Walls continues the storyline of Tennos as you explore a new area within the Necralisk and learn more about Albrecht Entrati, a famous Orokin scientist you may remember from the Heart of Deimos quest; and the mysterious unknowable Man in the Wall. 

Warframe: Whispers In The Walls Weapons

Whispers in the Walls expansion introduces a new secondary weapon class called Grimoire. It's a magic book that you can harness for void ranged attacks. 

Grimoire is the only new weapon confirmed for Whispers in the Walls so far.

Warframe: Whispers In The Walls Factions

Whispers in the Walls introduces two new enemy factions to Warframe. These include the expanded Necramech enemies, and the mysterious Murmur, created by the Man in the Wall.

Details on both these factions are pretty scarce at this point, but the TennoCon trailer did show an encounter with a Murmur boss fight called The Fragmented One.

That's everything you need to know about Warframe: Whispers in the Walls. We plan to keep this article updated with the latest info on this expansion, so don't forget to check back once in a while to catch up on everything Whispers in the Walls.
