Beverly Archer (born July 19, 1948) is an actress from the United States.

She grew up in California after being born in Oak Park, Illinois. She majored in theatre at both San Francisco State University and UC Santa Barbara after graduating from Alverno Heights High School in Sierra Madre, California. Her television roles as Iola Boylan on Mama’s Family and Gunnery Sgt. Alva Bricker on Major Dad may be her most well-known. She had guest appearances on a variety of other shows, including The Young and the Restless, ALF, and the film Project ALF, among others. In The Brady Bunch Movie, she portrayed a stealing teacher who was apprehended by Bobby Brady. She also played Miss Hardaway, a sexually repressed librarian and abstinence counsellor who is in love with Bud, in two episodes of Married… with Children. She also featured in a 1994 episode of Full House, in which she portrayed an unfriendly SAT test monitor. She appeared in episodes of Grace Under Fire, Family Ties, and The Fall Guy as a guest star. In The Nancy Walker Show, she played Nancy Walker’s daughter. Archer authored episodes for both Mama’s Family and ALF in addition to performing. She left the company in the year 2000.
