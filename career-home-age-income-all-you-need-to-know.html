Mark Walter is a successful businessman and sports team owner who has a net worth of $5.79 billion as of December 10, 2023. He is also the owner of the Los Angeles Dodgers baseball team and a major shareholder of Beyond Meat and Carvana. In this article, we will explore his career, home, age, income, and all you need to know about him.

Mark Walter Net Worth

Career

Mark Walter was born on January 1, 1960 in Cedar Rapids, Iowa. He graduated from Creighton University with a degree in economics and then earned his law degree from Northwestern University. He started his career as an investment banker at Goldman Sachs in Chicago before joining Liberty Hampshire Capital Partners in 1994.

In 1998, he founded Guggenheim Partners with Michael Bloomberg and other partners. The firm grew rapidly and expanded into various sectors such as real estate, private equity, hedge funds, credit markets, and alternative investments. Walter became the CEO of Guggenheim Partners in 2006 and led the firm through several acquisitions and mergers.

Some of the notable deals that Mark Walter was involved in include:

  • The acquisition of Fortress Investment Group for $4 billion in 2007
  • The merger of Fortress Investment Group with Blackstone Group for $26 billion in 2007
  • The acquisition of Credit Suisse First Boston for $50 billion in 2008
  • The merger of Credit Suisse First Boston with Merrill Lynch for $50 billion in 2008
  • The acquisition of Barclays Global Investors for $5.4 billion in 2012
  • The acquisition of E*TRADE Financial for $13.5 billion in 2012
  • The acquisition of TD Ameritrade Holding Corporation for $26 billion in 2018

Walter also invested heavily in sports teams through his ownership group that includes Magic Johnson. He bought the Los Angeles Dodgers baseball team for $2.2 billion from Frank McCourt in 2012 and became their majority owner after a long legal battle. He also owns a minority stake in the Miami Heat basketball team since 2014.
