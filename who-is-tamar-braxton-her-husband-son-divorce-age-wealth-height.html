Who is Tamar Braxton?Her husband, son, divorce, age, wealth, height

September 2024 · 4 minute read

Tamar Braxton is an American singer, best known for being the sister of Toni Braxton and a member of the R&B singing group The Braxtons.

Tamar, who has not found much success in a solo singing career, went on to star in her own reality television series while also becoming a TV personality. A former co-host of the Real talk show, she has even ventured into business, launching the Tamar collection, a ready-to-wear and footwear line.

Tamar Braxton Biography, Age

Tamar Braxton was born on Tamar Estine Braxton17. March 1977, the youngest of six children born to Evelyn Jackson and Micheal Conrad Braxton Sr. in Severn, Maryland, USA. Her father was a pastor at the local church but also worked in an electricity company, while her mother was an amateur opera singer and beautician.

Tamar and her siblings; Toni, Michael Jr., Traci, Towanda and Trina all grew up in a strictly religious household. She began singing as a toddler, following in the footsteps of her mother and sisters. Like her sisters, she joined the choir at her local church, where her father served. There, the Braxton girls gained popularity and performed together as The Braxtons.

In 1989 they were co-signed with AristaA year later they released their first single entitled “Good Life”. The song was a commercial failure and caused them to be dropped. However, it helped advance Toni’s career as an older sister. Tamar became a backup singer for her sister and appeared in music videos alongside her.

In 1996, Atlantic Records offered the group a record deal, this time without Toni and Traci. That year they released the album “So Many Ways” with the single of the same name. The album fared better than their first project together, peaking at number 26 on the Billboard R&B/Hip-Hop Albums chart. A world tour with Toni Braxton, opening for them, followed before the group disbanded after Tamar decided to pursue a solo career.

Tamar Braxton released a self-titled album in 2000 after signing with DreamWorks Records that same year. The album featured production work from Missy Elliot and Jermaine Dupri but still did not receive radio play, resulting in it being dropped. She unsuccessfully attempted to release another album in 2004 after signing on to Casablanca Records. Only two singles were released to the public.

After a six-year hiatus, Tamar’s music was included on the Adidas 2: The Music Compilation. That same year she reunited with her sisters and mother for a reality television series on WEtv called “Braxton Family Values”. The success of the reality series led to a spin-off series starring her and her husband that focused on her married life and solo career.

Tamar Braxton became a bit of a StarReality TV when she started to gain a following. In 2013 she secured a record deal with Epic Records and officially released her second album entitled Love and War. The album hit shelves on September 3, 2013 and sold 114,000 copies in its first week. Its singles topped the charts, with the title track itself reaching #1 on the Adult R&B Songs chart, staying there for nine weeks. That same year, she co-hosted Fox Broadcasting Company’s talk show The Real. She was on the show for three years before being fired on May 22, 2016.

Wealth (income and earnings)

Tamar Braxton’s net worth is said to be around $7million. This number was calculated from her earnings as a television personality, singer, reality TV show actress as well as her other business ventures which includes a footwear and apparel line.

Family – husband, son, divorce

Tamar Braxton has been married twice. Her first marriage was to music producer Darrell “Delite” Allamby. The marriage lasted two years.

In 2013, the same year that she divorced her husband, Tamar started seeing another music producer and record executive, Vincent Herbert. The couple were five years old before they exchanged vows in 2008. In June 2013, the couple were blessed with a son, whom they named Logan Vincent Herbert.

Recent reports have indicated that the marriage, in one particular report, Braxton may file for divorce from Herbert in October 2017, citing “irreconcilable differences.” However, the report has not yet been confirmed, so we have reason to believe they are still together.
