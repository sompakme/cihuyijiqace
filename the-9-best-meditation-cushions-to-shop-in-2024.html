What should I look for in one of the best meditation cushions?

When searching for your next cushion, professionals recommend keeping an eye out for those with firm support, an adjustable height, and the ability to fit your meditation position of choice. "Some people who are on-the-go may want to look for cushions with handles that make the pillows easy to carry," Zucker adds.

What's the best way to sit on a meditation cushion?

Experts say that the best position for meditation is whatever feels most comfortable for you for the duration of your practice. "Sitting cross-legged or with knees underneath helps to engage a straight spine and keep your pelvis tilted downward, making your body more aware and present," MacPherson says. "Rigid positions can interfere with the overall experience, especially for beginners. Posture and position are important, but comfort is key to experiencing wellness in the way that works best for our students."
