Tyra Banks model career

She was born in California, into the family of a medical photographer, Carolyn London, and a computer consultant Donald Banks. Tyra has an older brother Devin. She attended John Burroughs Middle School and later switched to Immaculate Heart High School, where she graduated in 1991.

She made her first steps in her modelling career while at school. Her mother helped her to put together her portfolio, and later she sent it to four agencies. Unfortunately, none agreed to sign her.

Finally, she managed to work with LA Models and IMG Models, before the famous Elite Model Management agency marked her. At that moment she was only 16.

Tyra Banks movies and TV shows
