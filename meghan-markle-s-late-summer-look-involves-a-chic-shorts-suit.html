Meghan Markle's Late Summer Look Involves a Chic Shorts Suit

September 2024 · 2 minute read

The Duchess of Sussex’s incredible style has touched down in Düsseldorf, Germany, for the 2023 Invictus Games.

For her second day at the annual sports tournament, Duchess Meghan showed up at the Merkur Spiel-Arena wearing a chic monochromatic shorts suit. The late-summer ensemble involved a lapel-less white blazer with black trim from J.Crew, a pair of cream pleated shorts from Staud, and beige slingback flats with a black cap-toe from Chanel. She accessorized with a black Givenchy belt, diamond stud earrings, and her go-to gold Cartier watch.

Meanwhile, Prince Harry accompanied his wife in a casual outfit consisting of a black collared shirt bearing the Invictus Games logo, black jeans, and dark blue suede slip-on sneakers.

Yesterday, the couple hosted a friends-and-family reception at an event room at the Düsseldorf Airport. There, Meghan wore a pleated midi dress from Banana Republic Factory (currently on sale for just $70). The preppy piece features black buttons, a crisp collar, and mid-length sleeves. She elevated the look by cinching the waist of the dress with a braided black leather belt, then finished it off with black suede pumps. As for her accessories, she again wore her Cartier watch, as well as a colorful beaded friendship bracelet that read I am Invictus—a nod to the motto of the games her husband founded.

Harry coordinated with his wife in a sleek black suit, which he paired with a dark blue button-up and glossy black dress shoes.
