Unseen Footage of Andrade with Charlotte Flair Prior to WWE Royal Rumble Return

September 2024 · 2 minute read

Charlotte Flair might be on the sidelines due to injury for now, but she decided to be back on the WWE road for one night and her prime reason was in regards to former WWE superstar, Andrade El Idolo returning to the global juggernaut.

In an unseen footage, the 14-time World champion was spotted with her real-life husband backstage. She was seen helping Andrade with his tanner, spraying and rubbing it across his back, right before he walked out in a WWE arena after nearly three years.

El Idolo made his triumphant return in the 2024 Men’s Royal Rumble match, entering the bout at #4. He displayed an excellent showing on his first night back in WWE, before being eliminated by the Colossal, Bronson Reed.

Andrade El Idolo was a top mid-card talent for WWE before he left the company in 2021. He then signed with their rival promotion, AEW while also competing for other companies as well. However, he left All Elite after their Worlds End pay-per-view in December 2023.

With Andrade back in the global juggernaut for one more run, it will be interesting to see what WWE has planned for the top Mexican star to emerge as a marquee attraction for them.
