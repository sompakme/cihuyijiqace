Zion at 4: Wizkids Baby Mama Jada Dedicates New Project to Son on His Birthday

August 2024 · 3 minute read
  • Top Nigerian singer, Wizkid’s baby mama, Jada P, has celebrated their son, Zion, in a unique way on his fourth birthday
  • Zion turned a new age on October 28 and Jada decided to honour him by launching a new project
  • The happy mother noted that Zion’s birth changed her life positively and he is living proof of God’s blessing in her life

Nigerian singer, Wizkid and his baby mama, Jada’s son, Zion, turned a year older on October 28 and they have both celebrated him in a unique way.

Zion who just clocked four was honoured by his mother, Jada, after she launched her new project on his big day.

Taking to her Instagram page, the proud mother noted that announcing her project on Zion’s birthday means so much to her.

According to her, when Zion was born, a new chapter started in her life and he is her greatest gift as well as proof of God’s love for her.

That’s the energy I want to bring to the world with this project and I’m dedicating it all to my son. Let’s build a Legacy! "Happy Birthday Zion! #Zeeman.”
