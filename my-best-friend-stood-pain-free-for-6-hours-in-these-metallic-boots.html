My Best Friend Stood Pain-Free for 6+ Hours in These Metallic Boots

August 2024 · 3 minute read

My FOMO has reached new heights with all the concerts happening this summer, but I love how creative everyone has been with their tour attire. The Renaissance Tour, in particular, has single-handedly put metallic silver back on the map, even before Beyoncé officially announced that everyone should wear silver to the rest of her shows. My TikTok FYP has been flooded with crystal cowboy hats and mirrored-disco boots — my friends included.

Franco Sarto Katherine Pointed Toe Knee-High Boot 

I recently helped my best friend choose her outfit for the L.A. show, and I suggested silver sneakers since she would be standing in Club Renaissance, but she insisted on wearing boots to match her cowboy hat. And while I was nervous her feet wouldn’t stand a chance in the Franco Sarto Katherine Pointed Toe Knee-High Boot, she proved me wrong and was able to stand, dance, and jump in these metallic boots for hours. 

The boots also come in a graphite gray and in sizes 6 through 11 in medium and wide widths. They featured a pointed toe, a 3.5-inch block heel for added support and comfort, and a zipper at the back of the boot. It stops at the knee and features a rubber sole that helps with traction and grip on slick surfaces. 

Not only did these silver-wrapped boots take my best friend's look to the next level, she had other Renaissance stans buying her boots at the concert because of how long and comfortably she was able to wear them without taking them off. She wore them for over six-plus hours because of how comfortable they were — and this was with zero break-in time.  She was able to stand in long lines for merch, perform dances alongside Queen B, and wait an additional hour and a half for her Uber, totally pain-free. 

She said they “fit [her] calves like a glove without the usual gaping,” “the pointed toe doesn't pinch her feet,” and said the zipper made them easy to take on and off. She also mentioned that “the leather is buttery soft” and the heel is “the perfect height and thickness” if you are looking for lift but don't want to compromise comfort. Her final thoughts? “They are easy to clean,” and once she got home from the concert, she could wipe the scuffs off with a generic leather cleaner. 

Need an eye-catching boot that is comfortable enough to wear for over six-plus hours? Look no further than Franco Sarto’s Katherine Pointed Toe Knee-High Boot and some other metallic boot picks from Nordstrom below.
