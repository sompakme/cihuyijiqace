Grandma Looks Lovely in Makeup and Hairstyle for 95th Birthday, Netizens React: &quot;95 Ke? Cold Zobo&quot;

July 2024 · 3 minute read
  • An elderly woman has gotten the internet buzzing with a video of her preparing for her 95th birthday
  • She was seen having her hair done on a low cut and also had a lovely face beat that changed her look
  • Her age attracted netizens who wondered how she managed to maintain her young look despite being 95

A grandma who is reportedly 95 years old has stunned netizens with a video of her preparing for her birthday photoshoot.

In the video, she was seen making her hair into the popular okuku (Benin hairstyle). After the hair, she had her makeup done, which turned out beautifully.

She prepared for her birthday photoshoot wearing a red wrapper and coral beads on her neck and hands.

The grandma's age was a surprise to many who felt she appeared younger. Some people also admitted that she had good skin.
