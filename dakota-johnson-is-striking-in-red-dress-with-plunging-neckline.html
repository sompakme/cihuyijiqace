Dakota Johnson Is Striking in Red Dress With Plunging Neckline

August 2024 · 2 minute read

Dakota Johnson exuded goddess energy at the latest stop on her Madame Web press tour.

Working with stylist Kate Young, the star showed up to the Mexico City movie premiere Tuesday in an incredible vintage Luis Estévez dress. The blood-red piece featured halter straps and a crimson leather-trimmed neckline that dove so low, it nearly hit her belly button, providing the perfect opportunity to frame four tiers of diamond-encrusted Messika necklaces of varying lengths. As for the rest of the dress, delicate ruching details covered the bodice, while the skirt flowed down to the floor, pooling around the actor’s feet. She also accessorized with a large diamond ring and red beaded bracelets.

Hector Vivas//Getty Images

As for Johnson’s beauty look, she opted for a soft smoky eye paired with subtle pink blush and a glistening rosy red lip. She wore her long dark brown hair down, styled in princess curls.

Hector Vivas//Getty Images

Johnson has been on quite the streak of Madame Web press tour looks thus far.

Earlier this week, she attended the superhero movie’s Los Angeles premiere looking fabulous in a custom Gucci dress. The unique piece featured an interlocking web of encrusted crystals that formed the silhouette of a slipdress, which she wore with just a nude-colored bodysuit underneath.

Prior to that, she walked the red carpet for a January promo event for the Spider-Man spin-off, where she wore a sleek black skirt suit composed of a cropped blazer and a midi-length pencil skirt from Versace’s Pre-Fall 2024 collection. She paired the set with black pointed-toe leather mules from Bottega Veneta, plus an array of diamond and gold jewelry from Jessica McCormack.
