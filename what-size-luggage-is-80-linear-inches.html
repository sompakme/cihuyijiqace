Aside from that, how do I calculate linear inches?

Simply said, linear inches – or any linear measurement, for that matter – refer to the sum of your bag’s three dimensions: length, breadth, and depth. The linear measurement or linear dimensions of a bag whose height and width are 28 inches, and depth and width are 10 inches, is the sum of the three dimensions: 28 + 13 + 10 = 51 linear inches.
