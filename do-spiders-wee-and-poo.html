Do spiders wee and poo?

July 2024 · 12 minute read

Answer

Spiders don’t generate urine as humans do, instead create uric acid, which doesn’t dissolve in water and is a near-solid. In this manner, spiders don’t deposit distinct faeces and urine, but rather a mixed waste product that escapes through the same orifice (anus) (anus).
