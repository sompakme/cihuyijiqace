Why wont My Jazzy chair wont charge?

September 2024 · 4 minute read

Answer

The two indication lights on the off-board battery charger should be checked if your Jazzy scooter’s battery isn’t holding a charge. If the battery is charging, the green light will blink continuously; otherwise, a solid red light will signal that the charger has received sufficient power. When there is a power outage, press the “Reset” button to restore service.
