A Nigerian novelist, Ugo Egbujo, has shared a photo of his large family in their radiant attires and people loved it.

In a Facebook post on Wednesday, September 28, the man revealed that his father had one wife, "eight plus four children", and 16 grandchildren.

Family members in matching outfits

The family members all stood according to their height so the camera can capture everyone. At the front row were the grandchildren.
