CM Punk Fans Leave Incredible Reviews For Mindys Bakery After AEW All Out Media Scrum Rant

July 2024 · 2 minute read

The AEW All Out media scrum ended in chaos after CM Punk went on a blistering rant on Colt Cabana, Hangman Page, and The Young Bucks, brutally eviscerating them in the process. It turns out that some fans were also looking for a recommendation for a good Chicago bakery.

CM Punk’s bizarre rant towards Colt Cabana, Hangman Adam Page and the Elite led to a backstage fight between Punk, Ace Steel and the Elite which resulted in everyone involved in the fight being suspended, and the AEW World and Trios Championships being vacated.

During his rant, Punk was casually eating some muffins, which he later revealed were from ‘Mindy’s Bakery’ in Chicago. This led to a lot of fans actually leaving hilarious reviews for the aforementioned bakery.
