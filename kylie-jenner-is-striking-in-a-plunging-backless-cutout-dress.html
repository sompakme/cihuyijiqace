Kylie Jenner Is Striking in a Plunging Backless Cutout Dress

July 2024 ¬∑ 2 minute read

Kylie Jenner has undergone something of a style reinvention as of late. The cosmetics mogul has been stepping out in breezy, feminine ensembles that can only be described as the opposite of her usual sartorial preferences, which typically involve bodycon silhouettes. But for her 26th birthday, Jenner made a return to her signature sultry style.

Yesterday, the mom of two posted photos on Instagram of herself posing in her birthday outfit while aboard a yacht during the sunset. The incredible look consisted of a black skintight halter-neck dress featuring a plunging backless detail with long oval cutouts. She accessorized the statement piece sparingly, opting for a stack of delicate gold bracelets, a few chunky gold rings, and tiny gold drop earrings.

As for her glam, Jenner kept her makeup fairly natural, wearing light brown eye shadow and a rosy lip. She also slicked back her long black hair up into a high bun.
